Switch Swatch

Switch Swatch is a simple swatch generation UI made by Shaun Fox and powered by RainbowVis-JS.

How to Use
  1. Add your own custom starting swatches in the left panel (hex values only).
  2. Adjust the generated swatch count to the desired amount.
  3. Grab your swatch values from the list on the right.

Tip: You can also grab and rearrange your starting swatches.
